What is Boosting House?

We are a company that operates a safe and a top tier boosting platform for Rocket League. Our boosting services are currently accessible only for EU users. We guarantee the highest standards of online game boosting available with the help on our professional boosting team, customer-centric approach and easy to use interface.

How does boosting work?

When purchasing a boost, a boosting order is created in our system and a professional player employed by our company gets assigned to complete it. To complete the goal of a specific boost, the professional booster will use its experience and skill advantage.
